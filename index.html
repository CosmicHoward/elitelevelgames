Age of Ninja

Age of Ninja is a fantasy roleplaying game in which players create ninja with extraordinary skills to complete missions full of danger and intrigue. Included is a robust, expansive character creator, active combat mechanics, and a multitude of storytelling options that take place in a unique and fully-realized fantasy setting!
